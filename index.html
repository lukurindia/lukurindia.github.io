About Us

About Us

Lukur is a Farm to Fashion concept that aims at reviving indigenous craft clusters and providing livelihood by producing handicraft products from natural fiber produced by the farmers of Uttarakhand and crafted by the artisans of Uttarakhand.

Farmers have connected with us thorugh our FPO

Our Products

Our Products

The major migration crisis due to no sustainable livelihood opportunities in Uttarakhand, is leading to a demographic change which is ultimately leading to the extinction of the art, craft and cultural heritage of the state.

Clothing

We make all clothing products like Kurtas | Sarees | Suits | Shirts | Dhoti

Accessories

Our accessory range include products like scaffs | caps | mufflers | socks

Household Items

We make household utilities like bedsheets | table cloths | rugs | pillow covers | curtains

Decor

Traditional decoration items include artifacts derived from hemp cloth, handicraft items.
